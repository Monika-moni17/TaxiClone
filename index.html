About Us

Blue Cabs is a trusted online cab booking service that ensures your travel is safe, fast, and affordable. We are available 24/7 and operate in multiple cities with a fleet of well-maintained vehicles. Our professional drivers are trained, background-checked, and customer-friendly. Whether it's a short city trip or an outstation ride, we have you covered. We use advanced GPS technology to track and provide real-time updates. Booking with us is simple — just a tap away through our user-friendly website or app. Our mission is to provide rides that are safe, punctual, and comfortable. Choose Blue Cabs — Because your journey matters!

FAQs

How can I pay for my ride? +

You can pay with cash, Your credit card,debit card. Just choose Your preferred method when booking Your ride.

How can I contact my driver? +

Tap "Message" in the app and click "chat" to organise your ride with your driver. Your chat history will be deleted upon completion of your ride.

How to book a blue cabs in Madurai? +

To book a blue cabs in Madurai,download the Blue Cabs app,sign in or create an account, enter your pick-up and drop-off locations, confirm your booking, and wait for the assigned driver to pick you up.

Can I cancel my ride after booking? +

Yes, you can cancel before the driver arrives. Go to "My Rides" and select the cancel option. No charges if cancelled within 2 minutes.After that, a cancellation fee may apply.

Is it safe to travel with Blue Cabs at night? +

Yes, safety is our top priority always. All drivers are background-verified and trained.Cabs are GPS-tracked and monitored live.

What if the driver cancels the ride? +

If the driver cancels, another driver is auto-assigned. you will be notified instantly via app and SMS.
